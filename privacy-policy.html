Privacy Policy

Swap & Spell: Word Puzzle  ·  Last updated: June 19, 2026

Overview

Swap & Spell: Word Puzzle is a mobile word-puzzle game developed by Tinker Apps. Tinker Apps does not operate its own game servers and does not intentionally collect or store gameplay-related personal data on its own infrastructure. However, Tinker Apps chooses to integrate certain third-party services (such as Google services) that process data as described in this policy. Tinker Apps determines which of these services are integrated and, to that extent, may act as a data controller for data collected through them; Google also processes data under its own policies. Tinker Apps does not sell personal data for money. All Google services referenced in this policy operate under Google's Privacy Policy (https://policies.google.com/privacy).

Data Controller

The data controller responsible for this app is Tinker Apps (United States). You can contact us at tinkerapps.support@gmail.com.

Data We Collect

Tinker Apps does not operate its own game servers and does not intentionally collect or store gameplay-related personal data on its own infrastructure. Your game progress — including level stars, level progress and high scores, coins, power-up inventory, daily puzzle status, an internal randomly-generated game identifier, and settings — is stored locally on your device using standard application storage.

If you sign in to Google Play Games, your progress is also saved to the cloud using Google Play Saved Games so it can be restored after a reinstall or on a new device (see Google Play Games Services below). If you do not sign in, your progress stays only on your device, and clearing the app's data or uninstalling the app will erase it (a non-consumable Remove Ads purchase can still be restored through Google Play — see below). Game data is designed not to include directly identifying personal information.

If you contact us directly — for example, by email for support or to make a privacy request — we will receive the information you choose to provide, such as your email address and the contents of your message, and we use it only to respond to and handle your request.

Google AdMob

Swap & Spell displays rewarded and interstitial ads served by Google AdMob. If an ad is shown, AdMob may collect:

  • Device identifiers, including the Android Advertising ID
  • IP address and approximate location
  • Ad interaction data

This data is collected by AdMob and shared with Google and its advertising partners for ad targeting and measurement. You can opt out of personalised advertising and limit certain ad-related data collection in your device settings under Google > Ads > Opt out of Ads Personalisation. AdMob is operated by Google under Google's Privacy Policy.

Ads in the EEA, UK & Switzerland

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), the United Kingdom, or Switzerland, Swap & Spell uses Google's User Messaging Platform (UMP) to present a consent message before any personalised advertising. Depending on the choice you make:

  • If you consent, AdMob may serve personalised ads and process data as described in the AdMob section above.
  • If you do not consent, ads are served as non-personalised — they are not targeted based on behavioural profiling or personalised advertising profiles. Google may still process limited contextual, security, fraud-prevention, or operational data necessary to deliver ads in accordance with applicable law and Google's own policies.

You can change or withdraw your consent at any time from within the app under Settings > Manage Ad Privacy Options, which re-opens the consent form. Where Google Play indicates that a user is a minor, ads are served as non-personalised regardless of consent (see Age Signals below).

Google Play Billing & In-App Purchases

In-app purchases are processed entirely by Google Play. Tinker Apps does not receive or store any payment information. All purchase validation is handled by Google under Google's Privacy Policy.

Swap & Spell offers a single in-app purchase:

  • Remove Ads — a non-consumable purchase that disables interstitial ads (optional rewarded ads remain available). It is linked to your Google Play account and restores automatically on reinstall, or via the Restore Purchases option in the in-game Settings.

The in-game shop also lets you spend coins you earn through gameplay on power-ups. These coins are an in-game currency only; they are not purchased with real money and have no monetary value.

Google Play Games Services

Swap & Spell optionally integrates with Google Play Games Services for sign-in and cloud saves (Saved Games). Swap & Spell does not create or maintain its own user accounts; signing in uses your existing Google account, which is created and managed by Google. Sign-in is optional — if you do not sign in to Google Play Games, your progress is stored only on your device and is not synced to the cloud, and Play Games profile data is not shared. However, other integrated Google services (such as AdMob and Firebase) may still process data as described elsewhere in this policy.

If you do sign in, Google Play Games Services collects the following for app functionality purposes:

  • User ID (Player ID) — a unique identifier linking your Google account to your in-game profile. While you are signed in, this identifier is also associated with the app's analytics and crash diagnostics (see below) to help group activity and reports from the same installation.
  • Saved game data (app activity) — your stored game progress, synced through Google Play Saved Games so it can be restored on reinstall or a new device.

Tinker Apps accesses only the Play Games data necessary to provide sign-in and cloud saves, and does not store it on its own servers. Google Play Games Services is operated by Google under Google's Privacy Policy, with additional information at support.google.com/googleplaygames.

Firebase Analytics

Swap & Spell uses Google Firebase Analytics to understand how players interact with the game. Firebase Analytics may collect:

  • App events (e.g. session start, first open, level start, level completion or failure, and Remove Ads ownership)
  • Device information (model, OS version, language)
  • A randomly-generated app instance ID
  • Approximate location derived from IP address
  • If you are signed in to Google Play Games, your Play Games Player ID, used as a user identifier

This data is processed by Google under Google's Privacy Policy. Tinker Apps uses analytics reports and aggregated insights generated from this data to understand gameplay trends, improve the app, and evaluate features, and does not use Firebase Analytics to directly identify individual users by name, email, or similar directly identifying information. In the EEA, UK, and Switzerland, Firebase Analytics does not store or use device identifiers until you consent through Google's consent message; if consent is not given, analytics identifiers remain disabled (Analytics may still record limited, non-identifying information needed for basic operation). You may also be able to limit certain data collection through your device or Google privacy settings.

Firebase Crashlytics

Swap & Spell uses Google Firebase Crashlytics to automatically detect and report app crashes and errors. When a crash occurs, Crashlytics may collect:

  • Stack traces and crash logs
  • Device state at the time of the crash (device model, OS version, available memory)
  • The level being played at the time of the crash, attached as diagnostic context
  • Installation and diagnostic identifiers used by Firebase Crashlytics to associate crash reports with a particular app installation, and, if you are signed in, your Play Games Player ID

This data is used solely to diagnose and fix bugs and is not used to identify individual users. Crashlytics is operated by Google under Google's Privacy Policy.

Age Signals & Age-Appropriate Experiences

To support applicable U.S. state app-store age-verification laws (such as the Texas App Store Accountability Act, SB 2420), Swap & Spell uses the Google Play Age Signals API. Where required by law in your region, Google Play may verify a user's age and provide the app with an age category (for example, an age range such as 0–12, 13–15, 16–17, or 18+) and parental-consent or supervision status.

Swap & Spell uses this information only to provide an age-appropriate experience — specifically, to apply more restrictive, non-personalised advertising settings for users who are minors, and to restrict in-app purchases for minors without verified parental consent — and for legal compliance and safety, as permitted by these laws. Tinker Apps does not use this age information for any other purpose and does not store it on its own servers. Age verification and parental consent are handled by Google Play. This signal is provided only for users in regions where it is legally required; in all other cases the app applies its standard settings.

Data Retention

Tinker Apps does not retain gameplay-related personal data on its own infrastructure. Retention periods for data processed by Google services are determined by Google's service settings and policies and may change over time. In general:

  • AdMob — ad interaction and identifier data is retained per Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Firebase Analytics — event-level data is retained for a limited period configured within Firebase; aggregated data may be retained for longer.
  • Firebase Crashlytics — crash reports are retained for a limited period set by Google.
  • Google Play Games Services — saved game and profile data is retained until you delete it via your Google account.

Local game data is retained on your device until you uninstall the app or clear app data in your device settings. Any support correspondence you send us is retained only as long as needed to handle your request.

Data Deletion

Tinker Apps does not store gameplay-related personal data on its own infrastructure. To manage or remove data:

  • Local game data — uninstall the app or clear app data in your device settings (you can also use the in-game Settings > Reset Game option).
  • Cloud saved games — delete via your Google Play Games profile or Google Account settings under Settings > Delete Play Games account and data.
  • AdMob data — managed by Google; visit myaccount.google.com.
  • Firebase Analytics & Crashlytics data — processed by Google. Some data may be associated with device- or app-level identifiers rather than your Google Account and may not be individually accessible or deletable by Tinker Apps. For information about Google's privacy controls, see Google's Privacy Policy.

Some data collected by third-party services (such as Firebase Analytics and Crashlytics) may not be individually identifiable or deletable, but is used in aggregate and retained according to Google's data retention policies.

To request deletion of data associated with your use of Swap & Spell, or for help with any of the steps above, contact us at tinkerapps.support@gmail.com. We will respond to and assist with deletion requests where reasonably possible, or direct you to the appropriate Google privacy controls.

No method of electronic transmission is completely secure.

Legal Basis for Processing

Tinker Apps does not intentionally collect or store personal data on its own infrastructure, but does determine which third-party services are integrated into the app and which events are collected. Where we act as a data controller in that capacity, we rely on the following legal bases under GDPR and equivalent laws:

  • Legitimate interests — Firebase Crashlytics is used to maintain app stability and security. Crash data is not used by Tinker Apps to directly identify users by name, email, or similar directly identifying information.
  • Firebase Analytics — in the EEA, UK, and Switzerland, analytics that rely on device identifiers run only after you consent through Google's consent message; until you consent, and if you decline, analytics storage stays disabled. Outside those regions, Firebase Analytics is used under our legitimate interests to understand aggregate gameplay trends.
  • Performance of a contract — Google Play Games Saved Games is used to store and restore your game progress when you choose to sign in, and Google Play Billing is used to process and restore the Remove Ads purchase.
  • Legal obligation — Play Age Signals is used to meet age-appropriate design and age-verification obligations under applicable laws.

You can change or withdraw this consent at any time under Settings > Manage Ad Privacy Options. Where consent is not given, only non-personalised ads are served.

Google may act as an independent data controller for certain processing activities described above. At the same time, because Tinker Apps determines which services are integrated and which events are collected, Tinker Apps may act as a controller (or joint controller) with respect to data collected through those services. Google relies on its own legal bases as described in Google's Privacy Policy.
